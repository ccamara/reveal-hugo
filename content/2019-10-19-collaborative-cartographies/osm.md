## Worldwide community

* ~5.4 millions of registered users
  - (~ 100 editing daily in Spain)
* ~ 4.500 milion nodes
  - (20.000 - 30.000 created every day in Spain)
* Data under Open Database License (ODbL)

Source: [osmstats.neis-one.org](https://osmstats.neis-one.org/)
